Our Story

Coffee has fueled the working world for generations- and it doesn't seem to be slowing down any time soon. Munzi Mocha wanted to be a part of that. After many years living on the Big Island and learning about quality Kona coffee- we made a plan to bring that full-body flavor to the heart of the USA. Our family takes pride in powering our local KC community with positivity and caffeine. Every day.
